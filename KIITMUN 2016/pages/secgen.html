KIIT International MUN: SECRETARY GENERAL

Message From the Secretary General:

“For, there shall never be a habit better than, becoming better!” The stage is all set to host something like never before. With 25 committees, 2000 and more delegates, 16 overseas collaborations, 3 explicit conference days, exquisite social nights, and a buffet of the diplomacy skills over words, we give you KIIT International MUN 2016.

I take the humble privilege to invite all the delegates, the stakeholders, the benefactors and the well wishers of this mega event to the 3-Day International conference. The event is supposed to realise the title of becoming the World’s largest MUN ever, in terms of registered delegate footfall and committees in its upcoming edition.

The standards that KIIT International MUN in its previous editions has set, is a legacy that needs to be maintained and taken forward. KIIT International Model United Nations 2016 will be the fourth instalment to what has been three excellent sessions of the same in the years 2013, ‘14 and ‘15. Coming this October and November, we wish to create new set of milestones in the journey of the MUN circuit in India with an expected Delegate turnout of over 2000 after having successfully achieved the milestone, of being the Asia’s Largest MUN in 2015; with a total registered delegate footfall of above 1800.

A successful event realises its meaning only when every entity involved in the same, participates and performs. Keeping such concerns in mind, our secretariat for the upcoming session has done a lot of homework to make sure that the MUNing experience is not just a robust experience for the experienced ones, but also for the beginners. The 25 versatile committees (including our flagship committees) have been crafted to fit MUNers of different experience levels.

At KIIT International MUN, we negate the doctrines that segregate participants of the basis of quantity of MUNs they have participated. We rather believe that the new comers are the real boost to the MUN circuit all across the globe. The true nature of MUNs is hence realised by the awareness of the people about the UN agendas, policies and goals. Promoting awareness among the masses is not just the act of accomplishing the values of education but also the much needed measure, to make this world, a better place to live.

The promise of realizing the title of becoming the World’s Largest is a dream that the entire KIIT International MUN secretariat has envisioned together. With the huge title shall arrive, the huge responsibilities. We also promise to deliver all those, with a smile and utmost sincerity. Your consent to this event shall the next leap in the long journey of making it a world class event.

The entire KIIT International MUN secretariat shall be available to deliver throughout the entire conference as scheduled and responsible to answer all the queries and issues, although we will make it a point to let none arrive. With all the blessings and smiles, we hope to see you in KIIT International MUN’16.

Sourabh Pandey
Secretary- General
KIIT International MUN 2016
