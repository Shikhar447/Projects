Al-Jazeera✖

  • Committee Strength: 60
  • Committee FAQ:
    Al Jazeera also known as JSC (Jazeera Satellite Channel), is a Doha-based state-funded broadcaster owned by the Al Jazeera Media Network, which is partly funded by the House of Thani, the ruling family of Qatar. Based at Qatar, this media news agency has 80 bureaus all over the world but Al Jazeera, being editorially independent, has been long disputed. Excellent coverage over the Middle East, Al Jazeera also has close ties with the United Nations through strings of organizations like UNCA and UNNGO.

Reuters✖

  • Committee Strength: 75
  • Committee FAQ:
    A part of Thomson Reuters Corporation, it has a major coverage over 100 countries and is headquartered in London. It has coverage over all the aspects of world geopolitics including the shifting political scenario in the United States, lack of humanitarian assistance to Rohingya and also the massive death toll in Syria etc. It is a very reputed media news agency and has been associated with the United Nations through inter-UN organizations like United Nations Correspondent Association (UNCA).

A wonderful Past!_

KIIT International MUN! Such a toungue twister it is. Before I was appointed for this job, I thought it was just another form of a jungle party. You didn't know! We have plenty of 'em. More than enjoying, I felt safer because animals were barred to eat each other on that day. As far as this huge event is concerned, I think they are good. They have people coming down from across the globe, the guys are busy carrying that 'swag' to match the beauty of the extremely gorgeous ladies. Yeah, I am emphasising. The secretariat that makes this event happening each time is a group of people who have worked endlessly to make KIITMUN'15, Asia's Largest MUN. However, I work more than anyone of them now! And now they are on their way to make it the largest in the world. Eh, the passion. In the last 3 years or 30 Chameleon years, these people have been visited by World class guests from variety of disciplines. UN dignitaries, visionary public figures and Government leaders have been a part of the list. Shhhh... I have a secret. I accepted this job to watch some of them.
